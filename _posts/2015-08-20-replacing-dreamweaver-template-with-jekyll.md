title: Replacing Dreamweaver Template with Jekyll
---

One of Dreamweaver's [greatest timesaving features](http://www.onlamp.com/pub/a/web-development/excerpts/0636920001393/templates-ch19.html) is "Templates" which is useful to quickly build similar-looking pages, and update every page in your site with the click of a button. 
You can achieve similar functionality using [Jekyll static page](http://jekyllrb.com/docs/pages/) feature to do so.
